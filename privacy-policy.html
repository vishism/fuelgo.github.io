Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Welcome to FuelGO. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how FuelGO ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use our mobile application and related services (collectively, the "Service").

By downloading or using FuelGO you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the app.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Location data – With your permission, FuelGO accesses your device's GPS location to show nearby fuel stations and current prices. You can revoke this permission at any time in your device settings.
  • Device information – We collect basic device details (model, operating system version, unique device identifiers) to provide and improve the Service.
  • Usage data – We collect information about how you interact with the app, including searches performed, stations viewed, and features used.
  • Account information – If you create an account, we collect your name and email address.
  • User-submitted data – If you report a fuel price or submit a correction, we record the submitted price, timestamp, and approximate location.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Display nearby fuel stations and up-to-date prices relevant to your location.
  • Improve the accuracy and reliability of fuel price data.
  • Personalise your experience and remember your preferences.
  • Analyse usage trends and fix bugs to enhance app performance.
  • Send you optional notifications about price drops or promotions (only if you opt in).
  • Comply with legal obligations.

3. Location Data

FuelGO requests access to your precise location ("while using the app" or "always", depending on features you enable). Location data is used solely to find fuel stations near you and to estimate driving distances. We do not sell your location history to third parties.

You can disable location access at any time through your device's Settings → Privacy → Location Services. Note that some features will be limited without location access.

4. Fuel Price Contributions

Prices submitted by users are an important part of keeping FuelGO accurate. When you submit a fuel price, the price and station information become part of our community dataset. Your personal identity is never publicly displayed alongside a submission.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Service providers – Trusted third-party vendors (hosting, analytics, crash reporting) who process data on our behalf under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal requirements – When required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of FuelGO, its users, or the public.
  • Business transfers – In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you before your personal information becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

6. Third-Party Services

FuelGO integrates with third-party services including, but not limited to:

  • Google Maps / Apple Maps – for station mapping and navigation.
  • Firebase (Google) – for analytics and crash reporting.
  • Apple App Store / Google Play – for app distribution.

These services have their own privacy policies which we encourage you to review. FuelGO is not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal information within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it by law.

8. Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's Privacy

FuelGO is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your personal information.
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details below.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of FuelGO after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
